How We Met

We first crossed paths in school — classmates who bonded over shared lunches, exams, and a playlist of nostalgic songs. What began as friendship slowly grew into something deeper, rooted in laughter, trust, and a lifetime of small moments.

More than ten years on, we've navigated long-distance separations, different cities, and countless late-night calls. Despite the miles, our lives stayed entangled and our bond only grew stronger. Now, after a decade of memories and unwavering support, we can't wait to celebrate this next chapter with our nearest and dearest.
